Game Instructions

  • Use Arrow keys (left and right) to move the paddle
  • Press the "Enter" key or click "start Game" to begin playing
  • You hae 3 lives to finish the game
  • Each life comes with a 2 minutes timer
  • The bricks have diffrent colors and diffrent point values.
    • Color Points
    Red 10
    White 20
    Blue 30
    Pink 40
    Orange 50
  • If the ball touches the ground u will loose a life
  • Break all the bricks to will the game
